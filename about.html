About Me

Hi, I'm Jordan and I believe in tackling problems head on. I love learning, finding solutions and helping others.

I started my career in direct sales because I wanted to learn how to understand people. How my customers thought and felt drove how I could best provide for them.

But I realized I wasn't creating solutions. I wanted to be the one who could figure out the issue as well as the answer. I needed to keep learning.

After an introductory Python class, I started building skills toward a career in development. I had found a technical and creative outlet in programming. I fell in love with the structure and power and logic of it. I knew this was for me.

I started working with developers. I built games and more advanced personal projects. I'm teaching myself the foundations of modern development, and while I'm by no means an expert, I am someone who finds solutions.

I'm ready to take on a role as a developer and provide both human and technical insight. Let's work together.
